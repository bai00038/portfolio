How do you approach a new design project?

As a Web Designer, I create custom web designs that capture your brand's identity and engage your audience. With a focus on user experience, I deliver beautiful, intuitive sites that bring your vision to life.

What tools do you use?

I use Figma for UI/UX design and prototyping, along with Illustrator and Photoshop for visuals. For front-end work, I code with HTML, CSS, and CMS.

What industries have you worked with?

I started in architecture, designing digital content for industrial parks. Now I focus on education and creative tech, working on school tools and portfolio-based design projects.
